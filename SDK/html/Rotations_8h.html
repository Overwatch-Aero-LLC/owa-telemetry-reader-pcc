void EulerAngleLimits ( double  Euler[NAXIS])

Adjust the Euler angles so that they are in a sensible range. Do this by first noting that pitch cannot exceed -PI/2 to PI/2. Note also that when pitch does exceed one of these values the aircraft roll and yaw angles are changing. For example, consider a loop. As soon as the aircraft passes PI/2 pitch, it is on its back, and going the other way.

Parameters
Euleris an array of the three Euler angles in yaw, pitch, roll order.

References PHI, PSI, and THETA.

Referenced by ApplyRotationf().

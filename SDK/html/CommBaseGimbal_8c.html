NoGoCenteris the center of the no go range in radians for all gimbal axis.
NoGoRangeis the size of the no go range in radians for all gimbal axis. Zero is free-running. More than 2PI is do not change center or range.
MaxRateis the max rate in radians per second for all gimbal axis. Less than zero is do not change.
MaxAccelis the max accelerating in radians per second per second for all gimbal axis. Less than zero is do not change.
PosGainis the position gain in angle per second per angle error. Less than zero is do not change.
VelGainis the velocity feedforward gain, less than zero is do not change.
DerGainis the aceleration feedforward gain, less than zer is do not change.
Temporaryshould be 1 to prevent update to nonvolatile storage.
Factoryshould be TRUE to update factory parameters as well as main.

Create and send a camera command packet.

Create and send a camera command packet containing the field of view, focus, and exposure settings.

Parameters
pCommpoints to the communication interface data.
NetworkIDis the address of the avionics to send the packet to.
Cameraidentifies the camera to control.
HFOVis the horizontal field of view in radians, or (if negative) the camera zoom ratio. 0.0 is do not change.
Focusis the focus length in millimeters, 0xFFFF is do not change, 0xFFFE is infinite, 0xFFFD is trigger, 0x0000 is auto.
EModeis the exposure mode: 0 for auto, 1 for shutter priority, 2 for bright mode. 0xFF is do not change
Shutteris the shutter speed. 0xFF is do not change
Brightis the bright mode setting, 0xFF is do not change.
Flagsis the camera flag settings, set MSB for do not change.

Create and send a gimbal command packet containing pan, tilt, zoom and focus rate, and mode.

Create and send a gimbal cmd packet. The command packet contains the rate or position data for pan and tilt, the camera zoom rate data, as well as the operational mode of the gimbal.

Parameters
pCommpoints to the communication interface data.
NetworkIDis the address of the avionics to send the packet to.
Panis the pan position (or rate) in radians (per second).
Tiltis the pan position (or rate) in radians (per second).
ZoomRateis the zoom rate from -8 to 8.
CamTimeoutis the time to apply the zoom or focus, in seconds.
Flagsdescribes how to interpret the data.
ImpulseTimeis the time to apply to an impulse (seconds).
FocusRateis the focus rate from -8 to 8.
Flags1provides additional details on how to interpret the data.
